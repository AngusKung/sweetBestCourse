      哪一學年度修課： 100-1

      ψ 授課教師 (若為多人合授請寫開課教師，以方便收錄)趙恬儀

      δ 課程大概內容(以下內容由從課程網複製而來)
         Week 1:Introduction to the course; grouping; an overview of seventeenth-century
                literature and authors
         Week 2:The gender wars (1): Joseph Swetnam and Rachel Speght

         Week 3: Metaphysical poetry: John Donne, The Flea, A Valediction: of weeping, A
         Valediction: Forbidding Mourning; order of final presentations

         Week 4: Carpe diem poetry: Robert Herrick, 'Corinna's Going A Maying', 'To the
         Virgins, to Make Much of Time'; Andrew Marvell, 'To His Coy Mistress'

         Week 5: John Milton, Paradise Lost, Books 1 &amp; 2 (Genesis &amp; Satan)

         Week 6: John Milton, Paradise Lost, Book 9 (Satan, Adam, Eve, &amp; the Temptation)


         Week 7:John Milton, Paradise Lost, Book 12 (Fate &amp; Future); review questions for


         Week 8: Quiz 1 (1hr); Margaret Cavendish, introduction &amp; A True Relation


         Week 9: Margaret Cavendish, The Blazing World


         Week 11: Introduction to literature and authors in the Restoration and the eighteenth
         century; Jonathan Swift, Gulliver's Travels; intro

         Week 12: Jonathan Swift, Gulliver's Travels Parts 1 &amp; 2

         Week 13: Jonathan Swift, Gulliver's Travels Parts 3 &amp; 4; conclusions &amp; literary notes

         Week 14: The gender wars (2): Jonathan Swift, ‘The Lady's Dressing Room’; Lady Mary
         Wortley Montagu, The Reasons That Induced...Dressing Room; outlines for final
         presentations due.


         Week 15: The gender wars (3):Alexander Pope, Impromptu to Lady Winchelsea; Anne
         Finch, The Answer (To Pope's Impromtu)


         Week 16:Quiz 2 (1hr); Samuel Johnson, A Dictionary of the English Literature;
         conclusions; preparations &amp; discussion on the final presentation


         Week 17: Final group presentation &amp; discussion


      Ω 私心推薦指數(以五分計) ★★★★★

          絕對是五顆星!!!
      η 上課用書(影印講義或是指定教科書)

         (1) The Norton Anthology of English Literature. Ed. S. Greenblatt, 8th
             Edn.(October 1, 2005);

         (2) Handouts and digital/Audio-visual resources from the instructor
      μ 上課方式(投影片、團體討論、老師教學風格)

         老師會用投影片,並且會在上課後上傳至CEIBA方便大家下載
         每週第三節課最後會留時間作小組討論

      σ 評分方式(給分甜嗎？是紮實分？)


         Evaluation No Item Percent Description(以下部分是課程網上的內容)

         1 Classroom participation and teamwork:10%
         --&gt; Involvement in in-class activities and group tasks.

         2 Final group presentation  30%
         --&gt; Presentation on a self-chosen topic relating to C17 or C18.

         3 Quizzes   60%
          --&gt; Two quizzes (25%,35%) based on general comprehension and critical commentary on the texts taught.

         老師開學做課程介紹時說過她的學期成績幾乎都會給在A-~A+(但還是會有例外)

      ρ 考題型式、作業方式
         老師的考試負擔不會太重
         期中期末考都有30%的作者姓名以及文本的配合題
         名詞解釋:題目通常都有出現在投影片過
         簡答題
         申論題:期中考是前一週公布題目帶回家寫,期中考當天交;期末考時則是當場寫
         對於課程的感想:(10%)只要沒有文法拼字錯誤就10分全拿

         期末報告分為書面(40%)和上台(60%)兩部分,
         內容有很大的發揮空間,但是要跟十七十八世紀英國文學的文本有關
      ω 其它(是否注重出席率？如果為外系選修，需先有什麼基礎較好嗎？老師個性？
加簽習慣？嚴禁遲到等…)
         這堂課非常搶手,本系生都不一定能簽到
         開學第一堂課就有很多人加簽,最後是抽籤決定
         雖然是外文系大二的課,但不需要甚麼基礎;不過老師是以英文講課,若英文聽力
         能力不夠上課可能會比較吃力

         第一堂課會分組,每個禮拜組長私底下做出席紀錄,期中期末交給老師作評分依據

         老師人超好,很樂意回答學生的問題


      Ψ 總結
         這是一堂可以學到東西,負擔卻不會太大的文學課!
         很推薦大家來修
